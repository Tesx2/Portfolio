About Me

From an early age, animation sparked my curiosity and set me on a creative path. Today, I’m a versatile designer who brings ideas to life through both static and motion visuals. My work spans brand identity, logo design, illustrations, social media content, print design, and engaging explainer videos.

What began with simple YouTube tutorials grew into professional training and eventually a diploma in Animation and Graphic Design from the Kenya Institute of Mass Communication. For me, design is more than a skill — it’s an ongoing journey of creativity, storytelling, and problem-solving.

I’m passionate about helping individuals and brands transform their ideas into compelling visuals — whether through sleek logos, impactful posters, motion graphics, or interactive websites. Every project is an opportunity to push boundaries and create something meaningful.
